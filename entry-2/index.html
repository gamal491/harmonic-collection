First Quarter

(Also referred to as a "half-moon".)


One of the primary phases in the lunar cycle, occurring about 7 days after the New Moon phase. Exactly half of the Moon's visible side is illuminated by the Sun.
This lunar phase is symbolic of growth, progession, and renewal. It is known to be a time for meditation, ritual, setting intentions, or as a reminder to start taking steps toward one's goals and dreams.


There are eight primary phases of the Moon:

  1. New Moon

  2. Waxing Crescent

  3. First Quarter

  4. Waxing Gibbous

  5. Full Moon

  6. Waning Gibbous

  7. Third Quarter

  8. Waning Crescent
