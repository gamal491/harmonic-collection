Moon Phases I Saw This September

A Half Moon- Also referred to as the "First-Quarter" moon.


A bit of history...

The moon is illuminated by light from the sun. As the moon moves around Earth, the amount of illumination it receives from the sun changes, creating the lunar phases. The complete cycle of phases, lasts approximately 29.5 days, known as a lunar month.

There are eight primary phases of the Moon:

  1. New Moon

  2. Waxing Crescent

  3. First Quarter:

    4. One of the primary phases in the lunar cycle, occurring about 7 days after the New Moon phase. Exactly half of the Moon's visible side is illuminated by the Sun.
    This lunar phase is symbolic of growth, progession, and renewal. It is known to be a time for meditation, ritual, setting intentions, or as a reminder to start taking steps toward one's goals and dreams.

  4. Waxing Gibbous

  5. Full Moon

  6. Waning Gibbous

  7. Third Quarter

  8. Waning Crescent
