First Quarter

Taken on August 25, 2023 at 7:46pm


(Also referred to as a "half-moon".)

Exactly half of the Moon's visible side is illuminated by the Sun. In the first quarter, we see what appears to be the right half of the Moon.
This lunar phase is symbolic of growth, progression, and renewal. It is known to be a time for meditation, ritual, setting intentions, or as a reminder to start taking steps toward one's goals and dreams.


There are eight primary phases of the Moon:

  1. New Moon

  2. Waxing Crescent

  3. First Quarter

  4. Waxing Gibbous

  5. Full Moon

  6. Waning Gibbous

  7. Third Quarter

  8. Waning Crescent
