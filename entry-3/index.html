Moon Phases

A Waning Gibbous


There are eight primary phases of the Moon:

  1. New Moon

  2. Waxing Crescent

  3. First Quarter

    4. One of the primary phases in the lunar cycle, occurring about 7 days after the New Moon phase. Exactly half of the Moon's visible side is illuminated by the Sun.
    This lunar phase is symbolic of growth, progession, and renewal. It is known to be a time for meditation, ritual, setting intentions, or as a reminder to start taking steps toward one's goals and dreams.

  4. Waxing Gibbous

  5. Full Moon

  6. Waning Gibbous:

    7. The phase after the full moon, when the illuminated portion of the Moon begins to decrease. This phase is larger than a half-moon but not fully round.
    The perfect time to rest and regenerate. It's a period of self-care; to find inner peace and reconnect with yourself.

  7. Third Quarter

  8. Waning Crescent
