A Waning Gibbous



The phase after the full moon, when the illuminated portion of the Moon begins to decrease. This phase is larger than a half-moon but not fully round.
The perfect time to rest and regenerate. It's a period of self-care; to find inner peace and reconnect with yourself.


There are eight primary phases of the Moon:

  1. New Moon

  2. Waxing Crescent

  3. First Quarter

  4. Waxing Gibbous

  5. Full Moon

  6. Waning Gibbous

  7. Third Quarter

  8. Waning Crescent
