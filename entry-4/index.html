A Waning Crescent

Waning means the moon is getting smaller.


The phase after the full moon, when the illuminated portion of the Moon begins to decrease. This phase is larger than a half-moon but not fully round.
It is a time to look inwards and re-evaluate your goals. It's a time to feel grateful for what has been this month and re-adjust your intentions moving forward.


There are eight primary phases of the Moon:

  1. New Moon

  2. Waxing Crescent

  3. First Quarter

  4. Waxing Gibbous

  5. Full Moon

  6. Waning Gibbous

  7. Third Quarter

  8. Waning Crescent
